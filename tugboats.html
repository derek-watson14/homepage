Tugboats

For the last several years, I have spent time between travelling working for a small tugboat company in Washington State called Star Marine. Through them I have been given a new perspective on the Puget Sound while learning mechanical skills and seamanship. Some of my favorite photos are from working on the water in the Pacific North West.
